How to use ?

  • 1) Duplicate this questionnaire. WARNING: do NOT change questions headers, nor add space, nor punctuation.
  • 2) Push the creation of the associate Google Sheet.
  • 3) On the Google Sheet : File > Publish to the Web > Published content & settings > Entire page + Automatically republish when changes are made
  • 4) From the Google Sheet's url, extract the sheet's ID code:
    https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1ai3SnqmW6tmA8AKsDmjxxI1ey-Esn3m6jc0_JeCDQVg/edit#gid=1709706079
  • 5) Enter your sheet's ID into the field below > click Open
